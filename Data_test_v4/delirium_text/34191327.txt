34191327|a|Retraction: "Protective effects of microRNA-330 on amyloid beta-protein production, oxidative stress, and mitochondrial dysfunction in Alzheimer's disease by targeting VAV1 via the MAPK signaling pathway," by Ying Zhou, Zhou-Fan Wang, Wei Li, Hui Hong, Juan Chen, Yi Tian, Zhao-Yun Liu, J Cell Biochem. 2018; 5437-5448: The above article, published online on 25 January 2018 in Wiley Online Library (https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/jcb.26700) has been retracted by agreement between the journal's Editor in Chief, Prof. Dr. Christian Behl, and Wiley Periodicals LLC. The retraction has been agreed following an investigation based on allegations raised by a third party. A detailed investigation revealed that several image elements of the experimental data were published elsewhere in a different scientific context. Thus, the editors consider the conclusions of this article to be invalid.
