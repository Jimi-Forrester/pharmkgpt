3308296|t|Monitoring cyclic antidepressants.
3308296|a|Therapeutic drug monitoring of antidepressants can ensure that a reasonable amount of drug reached the circulation, can aid in dosage adjustments for treating endogenous depression, can avoid toxicity, and aid in the diagnosis of drug-induced delirium. This article reviews the justification for antidepressant monitoring, discusses sample collection procedures, and presents guidelines for the interpretation of data.
