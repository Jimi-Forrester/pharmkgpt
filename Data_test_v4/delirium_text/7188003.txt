7188003|t|Prodromal syndromes in delirium tremens.
7188003|a|Three cases of hallucinatory delirium preceded by prodromal symptoms of several months duration are described. The prodromes were reoccurring visual and tactile hallucinations which were misdiagnosed and ascribed to nonalcohol-related illness such as schizophrenia and disorders of mood.
