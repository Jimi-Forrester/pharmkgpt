18212182|t|Severe acute hyperammonemia after brief exposure to valproate.
18212182|a|The authors describe a case of acute hyperammonemia after the addition of valproate to the medication regimen of a patient on multiple psychotropic medications. The patient presented with acute mental-status changes consistent with delirium. With prompt intervention and supportive care, her delirium quickly cleared.
