16919608|t|Effects of apoE isoforms on beta-amyloid-induced matrix metalloproteinase-9 in rat astrocytes.
16919608|a|Matrix metalloproteinase-9 (MMP-9) may play a role in the inflammatory glial response during Alzheimer's disease (AD). Astrocytes can degrade beta-amyloid (Abeta) and extracellular proteolysis via MMP-9 may be involved. Because Apolipoprotein E (APOE) genotype is an important factor for AD, we ask whether various apoE isoforms can influence Abeta-induced MMP-9 responses in primary rat astrocytes. Our data show that apoE4 significantly dampens Abeta-induced MMP-9 levels, possibly by downregulating the Rho-Rho kinase (ROCK) pathway. Reduction of astrocytic MMP-9 by apoE4 may affect Abeta clearance and promote Abeta deposition in AD.
