28824863|t|Hypnoanalgesia for Dilatation and Curettage Pain Control.
28824863|a|There are many acceptable approaches ranging from light to moderate intravenous sedation or analgesic drugs that are used to provide pain control in dilatation and curettage. We report the use of hypnosis as a nonpharmacologic approach to control pain in this manner.
