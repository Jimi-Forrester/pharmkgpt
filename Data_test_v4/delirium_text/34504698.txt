34504698|t|Case series: Rare cases of thyroid storm in COVID-19 patients.
34504698|a|Thyroid storm is an uncommon life-threatening thyroid dysfunction which was observed for the first time among patients infected with Coronavirus 2019 (COVID-19). The diagnosis and treatment of the rare thyroid distinctions such as thyroid storm in COVID-19-infected patients should be critically considered alongside common treatments of COVID-19 infection.
