1608494|t|Italian Multicenter Study on Dementia: a protocol for data collection and clinical diagnosis of Alzheimer's disease. The SMID Group.
1608494|a|This paper describes a protocol for identifying patients suspected of having Alzheimer's disease (AD) which was used in a long-term multicenter study in Italy. It relies on a progressive elimination procedure to arrive at a clinical diagnosis of 'very probable AD', with a sensitivity of 0.96 in terms of 2 years follow-up data.
