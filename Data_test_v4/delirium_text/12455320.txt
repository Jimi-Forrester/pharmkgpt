12455320|t|[Preliminary experience with dexmedetomidine treatment of confusional state and hyperadrenergic states at an intensive care unit].
12455320|a|Delirium (confusion) is an acute, reversible and fluctuating compromise of awareness and cognitive function, a state that can increase morbidity and mortality. We describe four patients with delirium associated with agitation and hyperadrenergic states refractory to haloperidol but responsive to dexmedetomidine.
