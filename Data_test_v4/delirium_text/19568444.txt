19568444|t|Cocaine: recent trends in Northern Ireland.
19568444|a|A review of autopsy reports in cases in which cocaine featured in the cause of death in Northern Ireland revealed that there were 18 deaths between 1(st) January 1999 and 31(st) December 2007. Analysis revealed an increasing incidence of these deaths during the study period and this is compared to national statistics and those published by local drug addiction services and police.
