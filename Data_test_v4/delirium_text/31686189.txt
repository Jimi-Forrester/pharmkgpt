31686189|a|Erratum to: the article "Multicomponent, nonpharmacological delirium interventions for older inpatients. A scoping review." Unfortunately, on 18.10.2019 a preliminary and incorrect version of the flowchart (Fig. 1) was published online. In the corrected and final version one additionally identified record is listed in the flowchart. The records after removal the duplicates are n = 4451 and the records that are excluded after title abstract screening are 4571. The studies included are n = 25. In addition, a further exclusion category is added after full text screening (Study not available: n = 1) and an existing exclusion category (Repeated data use of the original study) was corrected to n = 1, which can also be found in the corrected online supplement No. 2.Z Gerontol Geriat 2019 https://doi.org/10.1007/s00391-019-01627-y Please note the correct version of Fig. 1.The original article has been corrected.
