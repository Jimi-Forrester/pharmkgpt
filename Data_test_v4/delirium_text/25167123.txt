25167123|a|People who develop delirium often require long hospital stays and are at risk of complications such as falls and pressure ulcers. They also have higher inpatient mortality rates. Therefore, Healthcare Improvement Scotland has designed a toolkit that includes a range of resources to support healthcare professionals identify and manage delirium in clinical settings. The toolkit is at available at tinyurl.com/kkcke66.
