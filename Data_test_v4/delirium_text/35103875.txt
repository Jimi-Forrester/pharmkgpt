35103875|t|4-Hexylresorcinol: pharmacologic chaperone and its application for wound healing.
35103875|a|4-Hexylresorcinol (4HR) is amphiphilic organic chemical and auto-regulator for micro-organism. As 4HR administration induces the stress on the endoplasmic reticulum, 4HR changes protein folding. The application of 4HR inhibits NF-kappaB signal pathway and TNF-alpha production. In addition, 4HR administration increases VEGF, TGF-beta1, and calcification associated proteins. As a consequence, 4HR administration increases angiogenesis and bone formation in wounded area. Strong anti-inflammatory reaction and capillary regeneration in diabetic model demonstrate that 4HR can be applied on many types of surgical wound.
