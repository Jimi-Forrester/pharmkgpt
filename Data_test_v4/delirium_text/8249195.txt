8249195|t|Unusual neurologic complications of typhoid fever (aphasia, mononeuritis multiplex, and Guillain-Barre syndrome): a report of two cases.
8249195|a|Although typhoid fever can frequently lead to such neurological manifestations as confusion, delirium, and encephalopathy, Guillain-Barre syndrome, aphasia and mononeuritis multiplex occur very rarely. In this report, we describe two patients with typhoid fever who developed these unusual complications.
