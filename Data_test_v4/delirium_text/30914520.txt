30914520|t|[For Who Is Comfort Set ?-In Order to Stop Inappropriate Drug Use].
30914520|a|In home healthcare, the usefulness of comfort set(also called emergency kit)is prescribed in advance in preparation for symptoms accompanied by pain. The common symptoms since the terminal phase are, pain, dyspnea, delirium(restlessness), excessive respiratory secretion(death rattle). It is essential for comfort care to change the route of administration of the necessary medication and to minimize distress necessarily by prescribing the comfort set.
