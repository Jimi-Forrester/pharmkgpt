32126139|t|Microglia in Alzheimer's disease: Local heroes!
32126139|a|In this issue of JEM, Reed-Geaghan et al. (https://doi.org/10.1084/jem.20191374) address the long-standing question about the primary source of myeloid cells located at beta-amyloid deposits. Using genetic labeling experiments, the authors identify resident microglia as the only myeloid cells present at beta-amyloid deposits.
