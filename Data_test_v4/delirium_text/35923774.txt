35923774|t|Turn-on fluorescent sensors for Cu-rich amyloid beta peptide aggregates.
35923774|a|Protein misfolding and metal dishomeostasis are two key pathological factors of Alzheimer's disease. Previous studies have shown that Cu-mediated amyloid beta (Abeta) peptide aggregation leads to the formation of neurotoxic Abeta oligomers. Herein, we report a series of picolinic acid-based Cu-activatable sensors, which can be used for the fluorescence imaging of Cu-rich Abeta aggregates.
