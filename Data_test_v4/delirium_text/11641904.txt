11641904|t|[Diagnostic problems in the case of dementia with coexisting hematological disorders].
11641904|a|The case report presents a 61-year-old female patient with profound dementia and coexisting hematological disorders suggesting Addison-Biermer's disease. Two possible diagnoses were considered; dementia of Alzheimer type or dementia secondary to pernicious anaemia. This case report reflects diagnostic difficulties concerning dementia syndrome in the presence of other causative factors.
