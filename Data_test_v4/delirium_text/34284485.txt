34284485|t|Trojan horses and tunneling nanotubes enable alpha-synuclein pathology to spread in Parkinson disease.
34284485|a|In Parkinson disease (PD), Lewy bodies (LBs) form in the gut or nose and spread into the midbrain. A study in this issue indicates that the spread is due to lysosomes "infected" with prion-like alpha-synuclein (alpha-syn) transmitting from cell to cell via tunneling nanotubes (TNTs).
