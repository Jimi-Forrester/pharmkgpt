9608414|t|A preliminary study of D-cycloserine treatment in Alzheimer's disease.
9608414|a|D-cycloserine is a partial agonist on the glycine site of the N-methyl-D-aspartate glutamate receptor. This double-blind crossover study of 15 mg D-cycloserine in Alzheimer's disease patients did not demonstrate clinical benefit. Higher medication dosage or long-term treatment may be required.
