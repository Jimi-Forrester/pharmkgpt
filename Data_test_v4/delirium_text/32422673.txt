32422673|t|[Dementia with Lewy bodies].
32422673|a|A dementia syndrome is often associated with parkinsonoid extrapyramidal motor symptoms. Established consensus criteria allow the diagnosis of Lewy body dementia - however, this diagnosis based on clinical criteria does not exclude the diagnosis of dementia of different etiology. In everyday clinical practice, mixed forms with concomitant Alzheimer's disease or vascular encephalopathy do frequently occur.
