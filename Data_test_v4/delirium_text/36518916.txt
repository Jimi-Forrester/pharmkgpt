36518916|t|Autoimmune meningoencephalitis associated with anti-glutamic acid decarboxylase antibody following COVID-19 infection: A case report.
36518916|a|Anti-glutamic acid decarboxylase (Anti-GAD) are associated with various neurologic condition; but no meningitis has been reported with it, so far. Evidence demonstrates the associated of autoimmune meningoencephalitis with COVID-19 infection. Here, we report a 44-year-old female with progressive loss of consciousness with anti-GAD65 meningoencephalitis 1 month after COVID-19 infection.
