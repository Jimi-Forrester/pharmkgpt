7858070|t|Varied psychiatric manifestations of acute intermittent porphyria.
7858070|a|Acute Intermittent Porphyria (AIP) is an autosomal dominant metabolic disorder that has various psychiatric manifestations. This is a report of a case who had six brief psychotic episodes of varying nature within a 2-month period. The psychotic manifestations included catatonic stupor, hypomania, and delirium in different episodes. The management aspects of the case have been highlighted.
