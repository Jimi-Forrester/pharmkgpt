31095107|t|JBPOS0101 attenuates amyloid-beta accumulation and memory loss in a mouse model of Alzheimer's disease.
31095107|a|Alzheimer's disease (AD) is a major neurodegenerative disorder characterized by the accumulation of amyloid-beta (Abeta) in the brain. Defects in Abeta clearance or the interference of Abeta homeostasis could result in Abeta aggregation. JBPOS0101 has been studied for its antiepileptic activity. It showed a neuroprotective effect and prevented memory deficits in lithium-pilocarpine-induced status epilepticus rats. In this study, we tested the effect of JBPOS0101 in an AD model. We showed that JBPOS0101 attenuated the accumulation of Abeta in 5XFAD mouse brains. Moreover, the treatment of JBPOS0101 rescued the deficits in learning and memory in 5XFAD mice. These data suggest that JBPOS0101 could be a potential therapeutic drug candidate for AD.
