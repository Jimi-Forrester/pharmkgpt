23994938|t|[Predictors of neurological complications in patients undergoing coronary artery bypass grafting].
23994938|a|The article describes current scientific views on the predictors of neurological complications caused by coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG) surgery. The authors present the results of the study clarifying the most important predictors of stroke and postoperative delirium. The predictive value of preoperative data of neuropsychological testing in the identification of risk factors for postoperative long-term cognitive decline in patients undergoing CABG has been shown.
