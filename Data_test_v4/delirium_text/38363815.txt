38363815|t|Pitfalls of Amyloid-Beta PET: Comparisons With 18 F-MK-6240 and 18 F-THK5351 PET.
38363815|a|ABSTRACT: We present 3 patients as pitfalls of amyloid-beta (Abeta) PET, who underwent 11 C-PiB (Abeta), 18 F-MK-6240 (Alzheimer disease [AD]-tau), and 18 F-THK5351 (astrogliosis) PET examinations. Despite negligible or tiny Abeta pathology, patients 1 and 2 were diagnosed with AD as the cause of symptoms. Despite widespread Abeta pathology, patient 3 was not diagnosed with AD as the cause of symptoms. However, if we had only conducted Abeta PET, patients 1 and 2 might not have been diagnosed with AD, whereas patient 3 might have been diagnosed with AD. Hence, both Abeta and AD-tau assessments are necessary to relate clinical symptoms to AD pathology.
