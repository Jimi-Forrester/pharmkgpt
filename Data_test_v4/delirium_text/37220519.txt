37220519|t|Fahr's disease presenting as Parkinson's disease along with dysphagia and dysarthria: A case report.
37220519|a|Fahr's disease, a rare motor and neurocognitive condition, is characterized by idiopathic calcification of basal ganglia. This article presents such case of 61-year-old female, exhibiting movement, speech, and swallowing difficulties with multiple calcifications in brain in NCCT. Early and supportive management can lead to improved outcomes and prevent unnecessary interventions.
