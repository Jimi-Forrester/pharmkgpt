3617246|t|Alzheimer's disease with refluxes.
3617246|a|This is a report concerning a unique combination of Alzheimer's disease with the following refluxes: buccosalivary, gastroesophageal, vesicoureteral, urethroprostatic and urethrovesicular, along with neurogenic bowel and neuropathic bladder. A second patient with Alzheimer's disease and vesicoureteral reflux is reported. The possible etiopathology of these unusual refluxes and their relation to Alzheimer's disease are discussed.
