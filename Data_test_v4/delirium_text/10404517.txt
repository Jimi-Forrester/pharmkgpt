10404517|t|[Prevalence of dementia syndromes].
10404517|a|A survey is presented of epidemiological data on the prevalence of dementia syndromes. Most studies have demonstrated that the prevalence index of dementia is doubled after the age of 60 years at intervals of 5.1 years. The data on Alzheimer's disease point out that its prevalence after the age of 65 years is between 1.9% and 5.8%. In the study of the population of the Mokotow City District of Warsaw aged 65-84 years the prevalence of dementia was 5.7%.
