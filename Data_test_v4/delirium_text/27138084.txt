27138084|t|Commentary on 2 Cases Involving Psychiatric Problems Following Organ Transplantation.
27138084|a|Organ transplantation may be complicated by a range of psychiatric difficulties. We present 1 case reporting that tacrolimus-induced catatonic delirium after liver transplant responded to memantine, and another case illustrating the need for ongoing psychosocial assessment and support after heart transplant.
