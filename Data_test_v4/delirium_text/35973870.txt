35973870|t|Escaping delirium: Designing an escape room active learning experience for geriatric resource nurses.
35973870|a|Use of a delirium escape room is an effective teaching modality that has demonstrated the application of didactic knowledge into clinical practice. Positive comments were shared by all participating nurses. In addition, this learning method was found to be effective for experienced as well as newly licensed nurses. This team building activity evolved into discussions about real life patients who experienced delirium and each group was able to connect the contributing factors with their patients. Communication, coordination, and collaboration were key components as to what lead to a successful escape for all in the end. Participant's evaluations revealed positive feedback indicating appreciation for this hands-on learning activity and "making it an enjoyable and memorable experience."
