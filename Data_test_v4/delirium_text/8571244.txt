8571244|t|[Acute intestinal infections and chronic alcoholism].
8571244|a|153 chronic alcoholics were given treatment against food poisoning. 55 of them developed withdrawal syndrome, 98 had alcoholic psychosis including 88 with delirium tremens. The acute intestinal infection in these alcoholics was managed with rehydration using polyionic crystalloid solutions under controlled diuresis. Previous relief of central excitation contributed much to a positive treatment outcome.
