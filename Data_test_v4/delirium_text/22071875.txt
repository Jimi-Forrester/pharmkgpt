22071875|t|[Update: studies in intensive care medicine. Results of the last 12 months].
22071875|a|Intensive care medicine plays an important role in the medical care of patients as well as the economic success of hospitals. Knowledge and implementation of recent relevant scientific evidence are prerequisites for high quality care in intensive care medicine. The aim of this review is to present an overview of the most important publications in intensive care medicine published in 2010 and the first half of the year 2011 and to comment on their attributable clinical relevance for intensive care practitioners. In 2010 and up to June 2011 many studies with high patient numbers have been published. The main topics were the treatment of respiratory failure, sepsis and investigations to improve analgosedation.
