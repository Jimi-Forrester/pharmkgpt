18282347|t|Development of a hospice formulary for psychotropic medications.
18282347|a|OBJECTIVE: The authors describe the concept of hospice formulary as is utilized at the hospice service of a university medical center. METHOD: A periodic review of hospice formulary, focusing on psychotropic medications and delirium prevention, was accomplished in 2006. This effort represents a multidisciplinary effort among hospice nursing, internal medicine, and psychiatry. RESULTS: An updated formulary adopting contemporary psychopharmacologic best practices was produced and implemented along with targeted in-service training to nurse clinicians. SIGNIFICANCE OF RESULTS: The modern hospice formulary offers opportunities to offer state-of-the-art psychopharmacological care and minimization of delirium through judicious use of psychopharmacological treatments for the psychiatric comorbidities common in terminally ill patients.
