14516372|t|Delirium in adults with intellectual disabilities and DC-LD.
14516372|a|BACKGROUND: No systematic studies of delirium in adults with intellectual disabilities have been reported. Reasons for the absence of research are discussed. METHODS: Two cases of delirium are described which meet the Diagnostic Criteria for Psychiatric Disorders for Use with Adults with Learning Disabilities/Mental Retardation [DC-LD]. CONCLUSIONS: DC-LD offers a diagnostic framework that permits comparison with premorbid cognitive function.
