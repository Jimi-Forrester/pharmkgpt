6846642|t|Psychiatric complications of multiple myeloma.
6846642|a|Multiple myeloma is an uncommon cancer, but of eight patients with multiple myeloma admitted to a general hospital over 5 months, five had psychiatric illness, and four of the five had delirium. Consulting psychiatrists should be aware of the common occurrence of impaired cognition in patients with multiple myeloma.
