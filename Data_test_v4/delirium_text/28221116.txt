28221116|t|Fungal Contamination of Methylprednisolone Causing Recurrent Lumbosacral Intradural Abscess.
28221116|a|Fungal meningitis transmitted through injections of methylprednisolone contaminated with Exserohilum rostratum affected 753 persons and caused 61 deaths in the United States in 2012. We report a case of infection recurrence after 24-months with the unique manifestation of an intradural fungal abscess. Fungal disease should remain on the differential diagnosis list for previously exposed patients.
