32671338|t|Translational neuroscience: the state of the nation (a PhD student perspective).
32671338|a|Many brain disorders are currently untreatable. It has been suggested that taking a 'translational' approach to neuroscientific research might change this. We discuss what 'translational neuroscience' is and argue for the need to expand the traditional translational model if we are to make further advances in treating brain disorders.
