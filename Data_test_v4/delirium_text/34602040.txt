34602040|t|Defining Vulnerable Patients with Heart Failure: Opportunistic Lessons from Covid-19.
34602040|a|Congestive Heart Failure is a chronic disease that can be associated with poor outcomes. Some patients are more vulnerable, while others who are vulnerable appear absent or silent to health services. COVID-19 pandemic is a good opportunity to explore this important area. This review focuses on chronic disease, heart failure and those who require greater consideration.
