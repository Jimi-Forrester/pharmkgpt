34005829|t|[Predictive Diagnosis of Alzheimer's Dementia].
34005829|a|Expanding technologies of early disease detection allow to identify Alzheimer's disease (AD) long before symptom onset. Hence, patients are increasingly demanding for these diagnostic procedures. Biomarker-based early detection of AD is therefore increasingly important in the clinical work-up. This article gives an overview of predictive procedures in the field of Alzheimer's dementia.
