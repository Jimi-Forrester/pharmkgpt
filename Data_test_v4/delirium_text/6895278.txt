6895278|t|Delirium induced by topical application of podophyllin: a case report.
6895278|a|The authors describe a patient who developed delirium secondary to the topical absorption of podophyllin; this may be the first known case of delirium after topical application of the drug. Other serious medical and neurological complications of podophyllin poisoning are also discussed.
